Privacy Policy for FocusWallah

Last updated: May 29, 2026

Dev Prakash Varma ("we", "our", or "us") built the FocusWallah app as a Free application. This SERVICE is provided by Dev Prakash Varma at no cost and is intended for use as is.

This page is used to inform users regarding our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information if anyone decides to use our Service.

We are highly committed to protecting your privacy. FocusWallah is designed with a privacy-first approach to operate entirely offline and locally on your device. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any of your personal data with external servers or third-party entities.

1. Information Collection and Use

FocusWallah relies solely on local, encrypted on-device storage (Internal Database) to save your app preferences, focus statistics, blocked app lists, and tasks. All of this data never leaves your device. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not request any personally identifiable information (such as your name, email, or phone number).

2. Use of Accessibility Services (AccessibilityService API)

FocusWallah utilizes the Android AccessibilityService API to provide its core digital wellbeing functionality. This permission is strictly required to monitor the active window on your screen to identify and block distracting applications, websites, or features (like short-form video algorithms) based on the specific shield configurations you have enabled.

  • We DO NOT use the Accessibility Service to collect, log, transmit, or share any of your personal data, screen content, or text inputs.
  • We DO NOT observe your actions for any purpose other than detecting when a restricted app or website package is opened so that the app can instantly block it and redirect you to the focus screen.
  • The Accessibility Service operates entirely within your device's local isolated environment. No processing utilizes the cloud.

3. Other System Permissions

In addition to Accessibility, FocusWallah may request the following permissions to ensure proper functionality:

  • Ignore Battery Optimizations: To ensure that the foreground shielding service runs consistently without being unexpectedly killed by the Android system while you are actively focusing.
  • Notification Access: To provide you with ongoing status updates about your active focus sessions and to clear distracting notifications if strict mode is active.
  • Usage Access: To fetch app usage statistics locally (screen time) to help you understand your digital habits.

All operations utilizing these permissions are processed locally. Absolutely no data derived from these permissions is transmitted over the internet.

4. Third-Party Services and Analytics

Because FocusWallah operates completely offline, we do not employ remote crash reporting tools, telemetry SDKs, or analytics services (such as Google Analytics or Firebase) that automatically send log data or usage behavior to servers. Your usage journey remains completely private.

5. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. Given that the app operates offline and does not collect any data, we do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted.
