Privacy Policy for FocusWallah

Last updated: May 29, 2026

Thank you for choosing to be part of our community at FocusWallah, built by Dev Prakash Varma. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.

This Privacy Policy explains how our app, FocusWallah ("we," "us," or "our"), collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our mobile application. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

1. Information We Collect

FocusWallah is built with a privacy-first approach. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number within the app.

To provide its core functionality, FocusWallah asks for certain on-device permissions:

  • Accessibility Service: FocusWallah strictly uses the Accessibility Service API to detect when you open an app or website that you have chosen to block, and to monitor your focus session. We do not use this permission to collect, store, or transmit your personal data, screen content, or text inputs. All monitoring happens entirely on your local device.
  • Usage Access (Usage Stats): We require this to check which app is currently in the foreground and track your screen time. This data never leaves your device.
  • Notification Access: If enabled, this allows us to intercept distracting notifications while you are in a focus session.
  • Draw Over Other Apps (System Alert Window): This allows us to display the block screen over distracting apps.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the data collected strictly locally on your device to:

  • Detect and block distracting applications and websites.
  • Calculate your focus scores and provide local analytics about your sessions.
  • Display your daily focus streaks and progress.

Absolutely no user data is sent to our servers. Your data stays on your phone.

3. Data Storage & Security

All focus data, analytics, settings, and task lists are saved locally in the app's secure internal database (using SQLite/Room). If you uninstall the app or clear its data, all your historical focus data will be permanently deleted. We do not maintain any cloud backups.

4. Third-Party Services

The app does not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs. There are no ads, and no data is sold or shared with any third party.

5. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. Because our app functions entirely offline and does not transmit data, it is safe for all ages.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
